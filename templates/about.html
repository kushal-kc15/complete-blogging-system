About InkSpire

A place to write, read, and think.

InkSpire is an open blogging platform where anyone can publish. No gatekeepers, no algorithms deciding what you see first — just writing, presented cleanly.

How it works

Sign in, open the dashboard, and start writing. Your post goes live the moment you hit publish. Readers can follow authors, bookmark posts, and leave comments.

Built with

Django, PostgreSQL, and not much else. The frontend is vanilla CSS with no JavaScript framework. Dark mode respects your system preference or can be toggled manually.

Who made this

Built by Kushal KC as a full-stack project — from database design to deployment on Render.
